A Mission-Minded Fellowship

At West Village, we believe the Gospel isn't just something we hear on Sunday—it's something we live out every day. We are deeply committed to being a mission-minded church, serving our local neighbors and expanding our reach globally to share the love of Christ.

Our congregation actively supports the international spreading of the Word and humanitarian aid. Most recently, our church family stepped out in faith to support a mission to Zimbabwe, bringing resources, physical aid, and the hope of Jesus to local communities.
