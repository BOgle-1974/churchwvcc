Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision: "A new command I give you: 'Love one another. As I have loved you, so you must love one another. By this everyone will know that you are my disciples if you love one another.'" (John 13:34-35)

Our Mission of loving God and others is to:

  • Bring people into a loving, real relationship with God through teaching and worship.
  • Help people find and fulfill their purpose in life and service to God.
  • Make the love of God tangible to our community through loving service.
  • Train and equip our church family for service and witness.

A Mission-Minded Fellowship

At West Village, we believe the Great Commission is a call to action. We are deeply committed to being a mission-minded church, actively backing works that spread the Gospel and provide vital humanitarian aid locally and globally.

🌍 Global Impact

Our global reach spans vital international ministries including healthcare, education, and planting churches. We actively support missions such as White Fields Overseas Evangelism, Cambodian Christian Arts, CMF International, and our recent project works in Zimbabwe.

🤝 Community & Higher Ed

We believe in sowing into our backyard and equipping the next generation. Locally, we partner with the Willing Workers Food Pantry, Clinch River Habitat for Humanity, UT Campus Ministry, and support Christian higher education at Johnson & Milligan Universities.
